## âš¡ Quick Decision Frameworks

### The 2-Minute Rule
*If it takes less than 2 minutes, do it now*

### The 80/20 Decision
*If I'm 80% confident and it's reversible, decide now*

### The Coin Flip Test
1. Flip a coin
2. Notice your gut reaction while it's in the air
3. That's your answer (the coin is irrelevant)

### The Mom Test
*Would I be proud to tell my mom about this?*

### The Hell Yeah or No
*If it's not a "Hell Yeah!" then it's a "No"*
- Use for: Opportunities, commitments, projects
- Skip for: Everyday small decisions
