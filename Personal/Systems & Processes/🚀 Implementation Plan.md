     * 5:30pm "Evening Shutdown"
     * 9:00pm "Update Habits"

   - The insight comes from patterns
   - Weekly reviews are non-negotiable

5. **Adapt the System**
   - These are templates, not laws
   - Remove what doesn't work
   - Add what you need
   - Your system should fit YOU

### âŒ Avoid These:

1. **Trying to do everything at once**
   - You'll burn out
   - Start with just Dashboard + Inbox

   - Simple > Perfect
   - "Good enough" system you use > Perfect system you don't

3. **Skipping reviews**
   - The reviews are where insights happen
   - No review = no improvement

4. **Beating yourself up**
   - Missed a day? Start again tomorrow
   - Habits at 50%? Better than 0%
   - Progress > Perfection

5. **Organizing during capture**
   - Capture fast, organize later
   - Inbox is for dumping, not sorting
