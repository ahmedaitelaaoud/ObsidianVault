## ğŸ§  PROBLEM-SOLVING & CRITICAL THINKING

> "We cannot solve our problems with the same thinking we used when we created them." - Einstein

### Structured Problem-Solving Process ğŸ¯

**For Every Problem:**

1. **Define the problem clearly**
   - What exactly is wrong?
   - What's the desired outcome?
   
2. **Gather information**
   - What do I know?
   - What do I need to know?
   - Where can I find it?

3. **Generate possible solutions**
   - Brainstorm multiple approaches
   - Don't judge ideas yet
   
4. **Evaluate solutions**
   - Pros and cons of each
   - Which is most practical?
   
5. **Implement best solution**
   - Break into steps
   - Take action
   
6. **Review and learn**
   - Did it work?
   - What did I learn?

**Practice:** Use this for debugging code, security challenges, life decisions

---
